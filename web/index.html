How does the AI forecast work?

Step 1 — Plot the data. We put each year's school count on a graph (x = year, y = number of schools).
Step 2 — Fit a line. The algorithm (Linear Regression) finds the single straight line that passes as close as possible to all the historical points — minimising the total error.
Step 3 — Extend the line. We simply read off where that line predicts school counts for future years.
